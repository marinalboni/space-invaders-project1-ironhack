FRY MISTER POTATO!

GAME MODES

Beat levels and save the galaxy from Mr. Potato.

New enemies will appear every few seconds. There is no way of winning so try to beat your highest score.

ABOUT THE GAME

This game was developed by Marina Laporte Böni and it is inspired by Space Invaders, created by Tomohiro Nishikado in 1978.

It was developed as part of my Ironhack Web Development Bootcamp. The technologies used in this game are HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript ES6. All the code was done from scratch.

The music from regular levels was created and produced by Amanda Cuervo, the boss-level, the you-win and the infinite mode songs are from FesliyanStudios.com and the menu song (Chopsticks) is from Jorge Hernandez.

I hope you enjoy playing this game as much as I enjoyed working on it.

Marina Laporte Böni - June, 2022.

HOW TO PLAY

IT'S AS EASY AS PIE ;)

  • PRESS W - UP
  • PRESS A - LEFT
  • PRESS S - DOWN
  • PRESS D - RIGHT
  • PRESS SPACE - SHOOT

BUT WATCH OUT!

  • Enemies will shoot as well - try to dodge their bullets.
  • Avoid the meteors.
  • If you see a heart or a lightsaber icon, try to catch it - they'll help you win.
  • Don't let enemies reach your space - you don't want to lose.
