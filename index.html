ABOUT THE GAME

This game was developed by Marina Laporte Böni and it is inspired by the original Space Invaders, created by Tomohiro Nishikado in 1978.

It was developed as part of my Ironhack Web Development Bootcamp. The technologies used in this game are HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript ES6. All the code was done from scratch.

I hope you enjoy playing it as much as I enjoyed working on it.
Feel free to share with your friends :)

Marina Laporte Böni - June, 2022.

HOW TO PLAY

IT'S AS EASY AS PIE ;)

  • PRESS W - UP
  • PRESS A - LEFT
  • PRESS S - DOWN
  • PRESS D - RIGHT
  • PRESS SPACE - SHOOT

BUT WATCH OUT!

  • Enemies will shoot as well - try to dodge their bullets.
  • The satellite will prevent you from shooting as long as you can see it.
  • Avoid the meteors.
  • If you see a heart or a bullet icon, try to catch it - they'll help you win.
  • Don't let enemies reach your space - you don't want to lose.
